About Me

I've been passionate about BMWs since I turned 16 and found a cheap one for my first car. BMW in general holds a special place in my heart for its perfect mix of performance and everyday usability.

After some time of owning one, I've only been more inclined to getting an M3 of my own. That inspiration led me to research all the different generations and create this website.

My goal with this site is to help others understand the evolution of this iconic car. Whether you're a longtime fan or just want to learn something about the M3, this website will be sure to teach you something new.

Here, I have researched each generation and collected track stats, specs, and even common ownership issues all to help you choose the right M3 for your lifestyle.
